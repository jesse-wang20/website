Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE)

I began my journey with SASE during my freshman year and ever since then, I have become a strong advocate for the AAPI community supporting diversity, equity, and inclusion at Stony Brook and beyond.

2020-2021 : Mentee

I joined SASE as a Mentee in 2020 along with 7 other mentees!

  • Aid the Secretary and learn more about Executive Board duties
  • Lead the mentee's in hosting a GBM
  • Founded and managed the Discord server where we can host events and interact with general body members

2021-2022 : Treasurer

Through special elections during my freshman year, I took on the role of Treasurer in 2021-2022, managing financial aspects of the organization.

  • Secured funding for our club (2021 - $1200, 2022 - $2200) which allowed a higher budget for events and a huge increase in memebrship
  • Drafted and finalized partnership package which led to a partnership with Skilled
  • Shadowed President to learn more about leadership and other soft skills necessary to be a good leader

2022-2023 : President

I became the President of SASE in 2023, leading the organization towards its goals.

  • Amplified campus involvement with the student body and other organizations through collaborations
  • Further developed my soft skills and especially grew in communication, public speaking, and accountability
  • Wrote meeting agendas for bi-weekly E-board meetings keeping track of collaborations, events, and new developments

2023-NOW : SASE Professional Logistics Coordinator

After I got too old (just kidding), I wanted to continue my involvement with SASE and looked towards other options. I joined SASE Pro which shared the same missions but focused on those in industry.

  • Worked with a team of Coordinators and planned SASE Pro National Conference 2023 in Atlanta Georgia hosting 700 Asian professionals
  • Managed session leaders and worked with speakers to host over 50 sessions during the event

Awards

  • Most Improved Organization - College of Engineering and Applied Sciences (2021)
  • Best Emerging Leader - College of Engineering and Applied Sciences (2022)
